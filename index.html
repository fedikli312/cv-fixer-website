Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

CV Fixer ("the app", "we", "us") helps you score and improve your CV. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

What we collect

  • Account data — your email address, handled by our authentication provider (Supabase) when you sign in.
  • Your CV — the file you upload and the text extracted from it, used to produce your ATS score, AI feedback, company-fit score, improved CV and cover letter.
  • Job descriptions — any job posting text you paste to check keyword match.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Pro subscription, managed through the app store and our billing provider (RevenueCat).

How your CV is processed

To generate AI feedback, an improved CV and cover letters, the text of your CV and any job description you paste are sent to our AI provider, OpenAI, which processes them to return the result. Your CV is not used to train AI models, and it is never published or sold.

Where data is stored

Account and subscription data are stored with our backend providers (Supabase). CV content is processed to create your results; we retain only what is needed to provide the service.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal data or your CV.
  • We never share your CV publicly.
  • We never show third-party advertising in the app.

Your choices and rights

You can sign out at any time from Settings. To request access to, or deletion of, your account and associated data, contact us at the email below. If you are in Türkiye, you have rights under KVKK (Law No. 6698); if you are in the EU/UK, you have rights under the GDPR.

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their data.
